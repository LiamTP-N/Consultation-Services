Daily Prescription

1. Sit-to-Stand

Strength

3 Sets of 8 Reps. Use a sturdy chair without armrests if possible.

Clinical Rationale: "Sit-to-stand power is a robust predictor of functional independence and mortality risk in adults over 60." (Alcazar et al., 2018)

2. Single Leg Stance

Balance

Hold for 20s each leg. Maintain proximity to a wall for safety.

Clinical Rationale: "Static unipedal stance performance is inversely associated with fall risk and all-cause mortality." (Springer et al., 2007)
