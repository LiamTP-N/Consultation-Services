Defining the Next Era of Powerlifting Research

Following a 25-year analysis of 60,000+ Olympic Weightlifting performances, I am applying a 'Big Data' approach to Powerlifting.

To ensure this research delivers genuine, practical value, I am surveying the community to identify the questions that matter most to coaches and athletes, from "bomb-out" risks to the junior-to-senior transition.

✓Talent Pathways: Avoiding burnout in junior lifters.
✓Performance Ceilings: Long-term trends in Raw vs Equipped.
✓Myth Busting: Testing coaching intuition against data.
