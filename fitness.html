"Surprise" Rounds

A random burnout burst dropped into the middle of your session. The screen flashes white with a 5-second "GET READY" countdown, then 30 seconds of one task - STRAIGHTS or HOOKS (chosen at random).

Slots fall anywhere within the middle rounds. In partner mode, each of you gets different surprise rounds at different random times.

Training Blocks

Split your session into two separate blocks with a longer rest between them - e.g. 12 rounds, 3min rest, then 4 more rounds.

Each block gets its own round count, work length, and surprise round count. Pick "2 Blocks" to configure Block 2's settings; "1 Block" for a standard single-block session.
