What is Range-of-Motion-Based Training?

The Concept: "ROM-Based Training", which adapts core principles from Velocity-Based Training (VBT) to the context of longevity-focused exercise interventions. Where VBT used speed as readiness to exercise, "ROM-Based Training" uses those daily variations in joint range of motion to create a bespoke and individualised exercise plan.

Instead of tracking speed or weight, the app uses your webcam to:

1

Assess

It scans your joints to find your squat Range of Motion for each day/session.

2

Exercise

It creates a bespoke workout target based on your baseline.

3

Guide

It uses spoken instructions to coach you through the reps, so no tech skills are needed.
